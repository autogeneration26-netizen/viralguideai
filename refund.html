Refund Policy

Last updated: April 3, 2026

At ViralGuideAI, we want you to be satisfied with your purchase. This Refund Policy outlines when and how you can request a refund for paid subscriptions.

1. Monthly Subscriptions

For monthly subscription plans (Starter, Pro, Growth), we offer refunds on a case-by-case basis within 7 days of the charge date. If you are unsatisfied with the Service and have not made excessive use of your generation credits, contact us within 7 days of being charged and we will review your request.

After 7 days, monthly subscription charges are non-refundable. You may cancel at any time to prevent future charges.

2. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, email us at support@viralguideai.com with:

  • The email address associated with your account.
  • The date of the charge you are requesting a refund for.
  • A brief reason for your refund request.

We aim to respond to all refund requests within 2 business days.

3. Cancellations

You can cancel your subscription at any time from your Paddle billing portal. Cancellation stops future charges but does not automatically trigger a refund for the current billing period. Your access will continue until the end of the paid period.

4. Non-Refundable Situations

Refunds will not be issued in the following circumstances:

  • Requests made more than 7 days after the charge date for monthly plans.
  • Accounts found to have violated our Terms & Conditions.
  • Partial month usage — we do not prorate refunds for unused days.
  • Repeated refund requests from the same account.

5. Processing

Approved refunds are processed through Paddle, our payment processor, and typically appear on your statement within 5–10 business days depending on your bank or card issuer.

6. Contact

For any questions about this policy, contact us at support@viralguideai.com.
