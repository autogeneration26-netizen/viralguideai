Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 3, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Genzco ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use ViralGuideAI at viralguideai.com. By using the Service, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account information: When you sign in via Google OAuth, we receive your name, email address, and Google profile photo.
  • Usage data: Content inputs you provide (topic, niche, platform, audience, language) to generate strategies.
  • Usage metrics: Number of strategies generated, plan type, and feature usage patterns.
  • Payment information: Payment processing is handled entirely by Paddle. We do not store your card details.
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, device information, and access timestamps collected automatically.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve the ViralGuideAI service.
  • Authenticate your account and manage your subscription.
  • Track and enforce usage limits based on your plan.
  • Send transactional emails related to your account or subscription.
  • Analyse usage patterns to improve our AI generation quality.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a third-party database provider with servers located in the European Union (AWS eu-west). We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your data against unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure.

4. Third-Party Services

We work with the following third-party services that may process your data:

  • Google OAuth: For authentication. Governed by Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Supabase: For database and authentication infrastructure.
  • Anthropic (Claude API): Your content inputs are processed by Anthropic's API to generate strategies. Anthropic may retain prompts per their API data policy.
  • Paddle: Our Merchant of Record for payment processing. Paddle collects and stores your billing information under their own Privacy Policy.
  • Vercel: For website hosting and delivery.

5. Cookies

We use essential cookies for authentication sessions (managed by Supabase) and functional cookies to remember your preferences. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies. You may disable cookies in your browser settings, but this may affect your ability to use the Service.

6. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will delete your personal data within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it for legal or accounting purposes.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Object to or restrict processing of your data.
  • Data portability — receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@viralguideai.com.

8. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

9. International Transfers

As we use cloud-based third-party services, your data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside your country of residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers.
