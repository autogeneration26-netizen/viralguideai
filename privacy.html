Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 12, 2026 · GDPR · CCPA · DPDPA Compliant

This Privacy Policy explains how ViralGuideAI ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal information when you use our website at viralguideai.com. We are committed to protecting your privacy and complying with applicable data protection laws worldwide including GDPR (EU), CCPA/CPRA (California), DPDPA (India), and other regional frameworks.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information: Name, email address, and profile photo received from Google OAuth when you sign in.
  • Content inputs: Topic, niche, platform, audience, and language you provide when generating strategies.
  • Usage data: Number of strategies generated, plan type, features used, session duration, and interaction patterns.
  • Collaboration Directory data: If you submit a profile — name, handle, platform, follower range, niche, collaboration type, bio, contact link, and country. This data is publicly visible.
  • Trend Alert data: If you subscribe to Daily Trend Alerts — your email, niche, platform, and country. Used solely to send daily trend emails.
  • Payment data: Processed entirely by Paddle. We do not store card numbers or sensitive payment information.
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, device information, OS, referral URLs, and access timestamps collected automatically.
  • Communications: Content of emails or messages you send to our support team.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide, operate, maintain, and improve the Service.
  • Authenticate your account and manage your subscription.
  • Track and enforce usage limits based on your plan.
  • Display your profile publicly in the Creator Collaboration Directory and Trend Alert Service if submitted.
  • Send transactional emails about your account, subscription, or billing.
  • Respond to support requests and communications.
  • Analyse usage patterns to improve AI generation quality.
  • Detect, prevent, and respond to fraud, abuse, or security incidents.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

We do not sell your personal data. We do not use your data for advertising.

3. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

For users in the European Economic Area, we process personal data under the following legal bases:

  • Contract performance: Processing necessary to provide the Service you subscribed to.
  • Legitimate interests: Improving the Service, preventing fraud, ensuring security.
  • Legal obligation: Where required by applicable law.
  • Consent: Where you have explicitly consented, such as for marketing communications.

4. AI Content & Data Processing

Your content inputs (topics, niches, audience descriptions) are processed by Anthropic's Claude API to generate strategies. Anthropic may process and retain prompts in accordance with their API data usage policy. We do not use your input data or generated outputs to train our own AI models without your explicit consent.

Important: Do not include sensitive personal information (names, addresses, financial details) in your content generation inputs, as these are transmitted to third-party AI services.

5. Creator Collaboration Directory and Trend Alert Service

Profiles submitted to the Directory are publicly visible to all visitors. You should not include sensitive personal information in your profile. You may request removal of your profile at any time by emailing [email protected] and we will remove it within 5 business days.

7. Third-Party Service Providers

  • Google OAuth: Account authentication. Governed by Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Supabase: Database and authentication infrastructure. AWS-hosted infrastructure.
  • Anthropic (Claude API): AI content generation. Your inputs are processed by Anthropic under their API terms.
  • Paddle: Payment processing and billing. Paddle is our Merchant of Record and handles payment data under their own Privacy Policy and financial regulations.
  • Vercel: Website hosting and content delivery.
  • Resend: Email delivery service used to send Daily Trend Alert emails and transactional account emails. Your email address is shared with Resend only when you subscribe to Trend Alerts.

We select providers who maintain appropriate data protection standards and enter into data processing agreements where required by law.

8. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase on Amazon Web Services infrastructure. We implement appropriate technical and organisational security measures including encryption in transit (TLS/HTTPS), access controls, and regular security reviews. However, no method of internet transmission is 100% secure and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside your country of residence, including the United States, where our cloud providers operate. Where such transfers occur, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place — including standard contractual clauses approved by relevant data protection authorities — to protect your personal data in compliance with GDPR and other applicable laws.

10. Cookies

We use essential cookies required for authentication sessions managed by Supabase. We do not use advertising, tracking, or third-party marketing cookies. You may disable cookies in your browser, but this will prevent account login and use of the Service.

11. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the Service. You may delete your account at any time from your profile page. Upon account deletion request, we delete your personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required for legal, accounting, or regulatory purposes. Collaboration Directory profiles are deleted upon request.
